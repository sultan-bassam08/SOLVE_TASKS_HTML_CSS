The Coding Academy by Orange was launched in June 2019, in partnership with Simploa.co, to offer a free of charge 6-month training courses in coding languages for free, supported by I-month internship in ICT sector. Every yeat students, out of thousands of applicants, get the opportunity to join the Academy after passing a very competitive admission process. The employability rate among the first batch of students exceeded 70% which proves the efficiene the academy's teaching methodology, curricula and academic staff.
